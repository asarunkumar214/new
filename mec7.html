OVERVIEW

The Department of Civil Engineering offers a 4 Year BE Civil Engineering Programme which is affiliated by Anna University. The course is designed to meet the demand of the modern civil engineers in Construction Technology, Zero Carbon Building, Structural Engineering, Geo- Technical Engineering, Environmental Engineering and Irrigation Engineering. The students are also trained to analyze, design and model the complicated structures and other activities of Civil Engineers. The strength of the department is the dedicated, qualified and well experienced faculty members who support student activities such as Technical Seminars, Paper Presentation, Aptitude Skills, Technical Quiz and Special Lectures on Innovative Areas.
